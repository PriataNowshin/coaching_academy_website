Our Regular Program

"Dr. Mahmud's Core Academy" will be tutoring students from JK to Grade 12. Our programs are based on Ontario Curriculum but will not be limited to it. The general subjects are:
  • Mathematics
  • Science
  • Chemistry
  • Physics
  • Biology
  • English
  • Tech.

Grade 1 to 8

  • Math, Science, English, French
  • EQAO preparation for Grade 3 and Grade 6
  • Special Entrance Tests Preparation For High School
  • TOPS Program
  • MaST Program
  • I.B. Program
  • SHSM (Specialist high skills Majors)
  • Leadership Program
  • Enrichment Program

Grade 9 to 10

  • Mathematics
  • General Science
  • English Language
  • Literacy Test for Grade 10
  • EQAO Mathematics for Grade 9

Grade 11 to 12

  • Physics
  • Chemistry
  • Biology
  • Mathematics Grade 11 Functions
  • Grade 12 Advance Functions
  • Grade 12 Calculus and Vectors
  • Assisting and preparing students based on their goal to getting into their desired Universities

University Program

We provide various university courses for undergraduate students to enrich their knowledge in practical terms for better future.
